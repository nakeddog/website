Services

The Naked Dog combines training, exercise and socialization to create a healthy, balanced dog. I offer off-leash hiking, in-home boarding and holistic training that makes your dog the best pet he or she can be!

Hiking

For the daily off-leash hiking service, I pick the dog up at your home, take them on a 1.5-2 hour hike, then drop them off at home, happy and tired.

Boarding

For boarding, I offer board and train or regular boarding in my home with a limited number of dogs at a time. The dogs get training brush-ups, daily hiking adventures and all the care, love and attention they are used to at home!

About The Naked Dog

Background

Established in 2013. A lifelong love of animals and several years of dog training led me to open The Naked Dog. As a long time practitioner of Natural Horsemanship, I sought to connect with dogs in a way that didn't involve treats or gimmicks, but instead worked with their innate psychology to produce the desired result. I firmly believe that regular exercise, strong leadership and proper nutrition are the key factors to having a long and happy relationship with your dog. This is a dream job for me and I am ecstatic to be getting such positive reviews from my clients!

Philosophy

What sets The Naked Dog apart from other training programs is the real-world training on the trails that's consistently reinforced every time your dog joins us for a hike. In addition to the structure of working with a trainer, the hikes provide daily exercise and socialization; dogs learn quickly from the good example set by the well-trained dogs in the group.
