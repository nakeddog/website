About

Specialties The Naked Dog combines training, exercise and socialization to create a healthy, balanced dog. I offer off-leash hiking, in-home boarding and holistic training that makes your dog the best pet he or she can be! For the daily off-leash hiking service, I pick the dog up at your home, take them on a 1.5-2 hour hike, then drop them off at home, happy and tired. For boarding, I offer board and train or regular boarding in my home with a limited number of dogs at a time. The dogs get training brush-ups, daily hiking adventures and all the care, love and attention they are used to at home! History Established in 2013. A long time practitioner of Natural Horsemanship, I sought to connect with dogs in a way that didn't involve treats or gimmicks, but instead worked with their innate psychology to produce the desired result. I firmly believe that regular exercise, strong leadership and proper nutrition are the key factors to having a long and happy relationship with your dog. Meet the Business Owner Allegra K. Business Owner A lifelong love of animals and several years of dog training led me to open The Naked Dog. What sets The Naked Dog apart from other training programs is that the training happens in a real world setting- out on the trails- and gets reinforced every time your dog comes out on a hike with me. In addition to the structure of working with a trainer, my hikes provide daily exercise, socialization and dogs learn from the good example of the already trained dogs in the group. This is a dream job for me and I am ecstatic to be getting such positive reviews from my clients.
